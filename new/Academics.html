Nursery Education

Our nursery program offers a warm and nurturing environment where children learn through play and exploration.

Subjects Offered:

  • Early Literacy and Phonics
  • Mathematics Readiness
  • Creative Arts
  • Social Studies
  • Physical Education

Standard Exams:

At the end of nursery education, children are assessed using internal evaluations to determine readiness for primary school.

Primary Education

Our primary education focuses on holistic development, ensuring that each child reaches their full potential.

Subjects Offered:

  • English Language
  • Mathematics
  • Basic Science
  • Social Studies
  • Civic Education
  • Creative Arts
  • Physical Education

Standard Exams:

At the end of primary education, students sit for the Common Entrance Examination (CEE) to qualify for secondary school.

Admissions

Welcome to Rhemafield Schools! Here’s how you can join our community.

Application Process

  1. Fill out the online application form.
  2. Submit required documents.
  3. Attend an interview (if applicable).
  4. Receive admission decision.

Requirements

  • Completed application form.
  • Copy of previous school records.
  • Two recommendation letters.
  • Application fee.

Curriculum

Our curriculum is designed to provide students with a comprehensive education that includes core subjects, arts, and physical education.

Scholarships
